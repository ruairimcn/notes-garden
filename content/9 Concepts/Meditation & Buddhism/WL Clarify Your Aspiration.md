# Clarify Your Aspiration
This is the first of [[The Five Foundations of Liberation]] in [[10 Literature Notes/Books/The Way of Liberation]].


It means knowing what you aspire to and aim for in your spiritual life, **not in the future but now, in each moment.**

What do you value most? Not in terms of moral values, but what is important to you. Contemplate this - don't assume you know it already.
