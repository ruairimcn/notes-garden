People generate a lasting first impression of others in seconds, here are some ways to make a good one:

- Pre-adjust your attitude
- Adjust your posture, stand straight
- Smile
- Make eye contact
- Raise your eyebrows
- Shake hands
- Lean in
- Ask them their name, remember it, use it
- Be human
- Be a good listener
